Slimamif Font - Шрифт Слимамиф

It has extended Latin and Cyrillic character set and can be used for Russian, Ukrainian, Belorussian, Latvian and virtually all other European languages. It has new Ruble sign and other special characters that are used on the web.

Stylistically it's thin, light font, somewhat between handwritten and art nouveau. 

Slimamif was designed by Dmitri Zdorov in 2016 and updated often.
It's free for personal use and free with attribution for commercial use.

For the latest version visit http://dimka.com/fonts
